Le Ba Khanh Trinh

I am a Research Engineer at Viettel Cyberspace Center (VTCC), where I work on Computer Vision and Artificial Intelligence. At Viettel, I've worked on eKYC, xContact.

I received my Master's degree in Computer Science at the Chonnam National University, South Korea, where I was advised by Prof. Hyung-Jeong Yang. Previously, I did my bachelors in Computer Science at the Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology, Vietnam National University Ho Chi Minh City (HCMUT), Vietnam.

Research

I'm interested in Image Processing, Computer Vision, Machine Learning, Deep Learning. Much of my research is about inferring the physical world (shape, depth, motion, object detection and recognition) from images.
